Privacy Policy — Evipedia Browser Extension

Last updated: 11 July 2026

This policy covers the Evipedia browser extension for Chrome, Firefox, and Safari. It applies only to the extension — not to the evipedia.ai website, which is covered by the Forever Healthy privacy policy.

The short version

The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything it does runs locally in your browser. No page content, URLs, browsing history, or personal information is ever sent to Evipedia, Forever Healthy, or anyone else.

What the extension does

The extension reads the text of the pages you visit only within your browser to find terms that match a known Evipedia evidence review, and highlights them. Hovering a highlight shows a summary card; clicking it opens the full review on evipedia.ai. This text processing happens entirely on your device and the page text never leaves it.

Data we collect

None. Specifically, the extension does not collect or transmit:

  • Page content or the text of pages you visit
  • URLs, browsing or search history
  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, etc.)
  • Health information
  • Location, authentication, or financial information
  • Clicks, keystrokes, or other usage analytics

Local storage

The extension saves your preferences — global on/off, "highlight once", and any per-site exclusions — using the browser's local extension storage. This stays on your device, is used only to remember your settings, and is never synced or sent anywhere.

Network requests

The extension makes a single type of network request: it downloads the public review index (reviews.json) from evipedia.ai — the same public file the evipedia.ai widget uses. This request carries no user data; it only fetches the list of reviews so terms can be matched locally. No other servers are contacted.

Permissions

  • Runs on all websites — the extension's content script runs on any page you read so highlighting can work everywhere. It only reads the page's text locally to find matches; the extension holds no broad access to your sites' data.
  • evipedia.ai access — the only host permission; used to fetch the public reviews.json index.
  • Storage — to remember your popup preferences on your device.
  • Active tab — to apply popup actions to the tab you're viewing.

Third parties

The extension contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. It does not sell or share any data, because it does not collect any.

Contact

Questions about this policy or the extension? Open an issue on the GitHub repository.
